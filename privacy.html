Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. We don't sell it. We don't abuse it. We just use it to make fovndX work.

01

Information We Collect

We collect basic information you provide: name, email, role (founder, builder, investor, mentor), and any content you choose to share on the platform. We also collect usage data to improve our service.

02

How We Use Your Information

To operate and improve fovndX, connect you with relevant opportunities, communicate with you about updates, and ensure platform security. That's it.

03

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your idea vault content is encrypted and only accessible with your explicit permission.

04

Data Sharing

We don't sell your data to third parties. We may share information only when required by law or with your explicit consent.

05

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data at any time. Contact us at fovndx@gmail.com or contact@fovndx.com for any privacy-related requests.
