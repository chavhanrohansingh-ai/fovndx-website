Is fovndX a social network like LinkedIn?

No. While fovndX has a feed to share ideas and progress, it is strictly an "Execution Feed". You can't post vanity metrics or fluff. Every post is tied to actual proof of work, making it an operating system for building, not just talking.

How does fovndX protect my startup idea when I share it?

We use a 2-Tier Protection System. You share the surface-level vision publicly to build hype. But your core IP, deep-tech details, and financials are locked in "The Vault". Anyone requesting access must sign an automated, 1-Click Digital NDA before you approve them.

Is fovndX an incubator or a VC fund?

Neither. fovndX is the underlying tech infrastructure. We provide the secure platform where founders, builders, and capital interact directly, without the middlemen.

What is the "Grit Score"?

It is our proprietary AI-driven metric. It analyzes a startup's or builder's actual progress (commits, design updates, live metrics) rather than their social media followers. A high Grit Score guarantees visibility to top investors.
